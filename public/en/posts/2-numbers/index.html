2 Numbers

2024-12-25 05:30 +0530

It finally happened. It was only funny when I was theorycrafting it but after it really happened now I don’t know how to handle this.

Have you ever thought about or even dreamt about time travel? Or something of sorts. Well, not exactly travel but some kind of phenomenon related to time travel.

It happened to me, well in some sort of way, It happened to her as well, the issue is only one of us knows. Well, technically, two because she counts as two. For fucks sake, Time travel is complicated.

So let me start this simply and keep it short.

I was messaging this girl I met a few days ago. She was funny and interesting. Right up in my alley you know and out of nowhere I started receiving messages via two different chats in my chat app. At first I didn’t really mind cause It was funny. But after a few days it hit me, slowly though.

In one chat, the most recent one, she was far more flirty and homely. It was likely we’ve known each other for some years. I brushed it off as her usual funny self but it was more than it. So, I became curious and as the idiot I am I asked her directly. Our conversation went somewhat like this.

Me - Hey, So. I was gonna ask you something.

Her - Yes, what is it now?

Me - Um, This might sound weird though.

Her - Okay, I guess. It’s not like we’re strangers or anything. And I know you so nothing can weird me out about you.

Me - See, that’s the thing. That.. ugh. Okay, Who are you?

Her - What do you mean? Is this one of your pranks? I’m redacted. She said her name.

Me - Okay, Why are you being flirty? We’ve known each other for like 10 or 15 days now.

Her - 10 or 15 days, what the actual fuck man. Is this a joke?

Me - No, No. not really.

Her - Oh, oh… you’re serious. Oh no, that only means one thing.

Me - What?

Her - No, This can’t be happening.

Me - What can’t be happening?

Her - Okay, you’re a smart guy. Right?

Me - umm.. I think so. Maybe.

Her - Listen to me very carefully. There has been a little issue with our time manipulator. Don’t think about it. It’s somewhere in the future and nothing to be concerned with. What I want you to do now is don’t tell the other Me anything. Can you promise me that?

Me - okay.

Her - That’s it? Just okay? No questions like What is a Time manipulator, when are you? Anything?

Me - I mean, If it’s something related to time, It has to be the future so I will eventually know about it. Less I know is better? Right?

Her - smart boy, that’s why I married you.

Me - Sorry what?

Her - Oops! Spoilers. I’ll fix this up so You won’t be able to message this chat anymore. It’ll remove itself from your device because of the reasons. But hey, It was nice peeking into the past. Save yourself for me okay?

Me - Wait, Married? What?

Although I sent the message, It didn’t deliver and I couldn’t find the chat afterwards. It was the most surreal few minutes I ever had in my life up to that point. I was gonna think that as a prank but a chat suddenly disappearing kinda made it convincing.

So, Married. Who would’ve thought.

Come to think of it, I will in the future.
