Writers block is the worst thing that can happen to a writer. It keeps happening to me. No matter how much I try to keep up with it all fails. I suddenly lose motivation, my writings get deleted because i forgot to save it or many others reasons that are completely out of ordinary. Still i try because once in a blue moon i write something mind blowing that makes me flip out and ask myself did i do that? So here I am for the nth time and hoping this would stick and hoping I can pull out some rabbits out of hat or at least a carrot. Before I go, 

> A person who loves you going to keep you to themselves that instead of asking the world from you, world ask you back from them.
