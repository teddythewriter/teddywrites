I just want to talk to someone,  
Or scream how much of a pain i am in.  
Even though I try to convince myself that this'll end  
It won't.  
It's not Physical pain to be treated.  
Everytime i see myself in mirror,  
I see someone who's struggling to survive.  
I see someone who's in pain,  
I see someone who's about to end it all.  
I wish i had the resolve to go through it,  
But i don't.  
I'm in an dilemma,  
Should i stay alive and suffer so it might get better  
or  
Should I just end it all and let myself be free  
It's hard when you have to build from scratch  
It's hard when you have to put up the bread for family  
It's hard to live with delusional parents who thinks that we're fine  
The Truth is we're not.  
They fucked up,  
They fucked it up all.  
and they are too delusional to admit it.  
I just want to leave and start over,  
So at least I'll be able to survive this mess.  
I know this doesn't make sense  
But,  
I hope you'd understand it.

Note to self - If you ever survive this, thank you for not giving up.
