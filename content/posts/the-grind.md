Dying is easy.

Contrary to what people say it's not a hard decision to take with all the shit that's going around. You can simply say that you had enough or you're worthless and you're left with no choice but end it all.

Do you know what's hard though? Living. No matter whatever the shit you have try living for a change. It's hard and you always want to give up but it's worth everything. When you achieve things in life you'll realise that you never would've felt it if you decide to end your life. That's what life is a well rewarding grind that you got to endure. Not everyone will go through it but to those who do you're awesome and keep doing whatever you're doing to make it happen.
